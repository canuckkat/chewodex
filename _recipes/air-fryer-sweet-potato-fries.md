# Ingredients

- [ ] 2 large sweet potatoes, cut into thin strips
- [ ] 2 Tbsp canola oil
- [ ] 1 tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 1 tsp paprika
- [ ] 1 tsp kosher salt
- [ ] 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

# Directions

Preheat air fryer to 400Â°. Combine all ingredients; toss to coat. Place on greased tray in air-fryer basket. Cook until lightly browned, 10-12 minutes, stirring once. Serve immediately.
